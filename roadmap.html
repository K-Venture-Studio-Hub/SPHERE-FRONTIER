Capture

Phase 1

Find and articulate your idea. This is about curiosity, observation, and turning vague excitement into something you can test.

  • Write down your idea in one sentence — what it is, who it's for, why it mattersDE Canvas →
  • List your motivations — passion, impact, mission, or obsessionRaison d'Être →
  • Identify what you already have — skills, assets, network, knowledge
  • Segment the market — who could possibly use this?Market Segmentation →
  • Pick one beachhead market — the smallest segment you can dominate first
  • Create an end user profile — demographics, behaviors, pain points
  • Estimate your beachhead TAM — how big is this specific market?
  • Name your beachhead persona — a specific fictional user

Documents for this phase

  • Idea One-Pager (1 sentence + 3 motivations)
  • DE Canvas — Boxes 1-2
  • Market Segmentation Worksheet
  • Readiness Quiz — Mindset section
🧪

Validate

Phase 2

Test your assumptions with real people. This is where most startups fail — by skipping. Talk to customers before you build.

  • Define the life cycle use case — how does the user discover, adopt, use, pay?
  • Write a high-level product spec — what it does, not how it's built
  • Quantify your value proposition — how much time/money/effort do you save?QVP →
  • Identify your first 10 target customers by name
  • Conduct 10+ customer interviews — listen, don't pitch
  • Define your Core — what can you do uniquely better than anyone?
  • Map your competitive moats — network effects, switching costs, brand
  • Run a crash test — smallest possible experiment to validate demand

Documents for this phase

  • DE Canvas — Boxes 3-5
  • Customer Interview Script (5 questions)
  • Unit Economics Calculator
  • Crash Test Protocol
🏗️

Build

Phase 3

Create your Minimum Viable Business Product. Not just a product — a business that works end to end, even if tiny.

  • Define the Decision-Making Unit — who says yes, who influences, who vetoes?
  • Map the customer acquisition process — how do they find you?
  • Choose your business model — subscription, marketplace, SaaS, licensing...Business Model →
  • Set your pricing framework — what you charge and whyPricing →
  • Calculate Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)LTV Calc →
  • Design your scalable revenue engine
  • Estimate Cost of Customer Acquisition (COCA)COCA →
  • Build your Minimum Viable Business Product (MVBP)
  • Prove that dogs will eat the dog food — get real usage, not just signups

Documents for this phase

  • DE Canvas — Boxes 6-9
  • P&L Template
  • 5-Year Forecast
  • MVBP Specification
🚀

Scale

Phase 4

Expand beyond the beachhead. This is about sustainable growth, follow-on markets, and building something that outlasts you.

  • Identify follow-on markets — where do you go after the beachhead?
  • Create your product plan — what's next after MVBP?
  • Build a financial model — P&L, cash flow, 5-year forecastFinance →
  • Prepare your pitch — narrative, data, visionPitch Guide →
  • Define your scaling strategy — partnerships, channels, geography
  • Set up tracking metrics — what numbers tell you if you're winning?
  • Establish governance — board, advisors, decision frameworks
  • Plan fundraising — how much, from whom, on what terms

Documents for this phase

  • DE Canvas — Box 10
  • Full Financial Model
  • Pitch Deck Recommendations
  • Fundraising Strategy Template
